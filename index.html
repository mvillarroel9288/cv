Miranda Villarroel

Founder and director of Omi

Miranda is the founder and director of Omi, which is a blog that explores literature, arts, and culture. She has interned at Cinema Verde which focuses on environmental advocacy. At Cinema Verde, she updated their news section daily as well as managed their social media platforms. Also, she interned at Peaceful Paths and served as an affordable housing advocate for domestic abuse survivors. There, she worked directly with survivors and helped them with legal matters to find housing. She now combines her passion for technology and human rights advocacy to help people gain access to clean water, education, food, and other resources all around the world. When she is not programming, she can be found writing creatively, reading, painting, hiking, doing yoga, meditating, learning a new language, or spending time with her cat, Minushka.
